Welcome to the Step 3 CCS Course

UWorld's Step 3 CCS course intends to provide Step 3 candidates with optimum guidance for clinical case simulations. This course includes all the most common clinical cases. Each case in this course has been solved step by step in a systematic manner compatible with the actual exam software. Physical findings, investigations, and management options are detailed with the most updated information. After studying these cases carefully, the candidate will have learnt precisely what knowledge and strategy is needed to confidently and successfully complete each examination. This course also provides first hand helpful information that no other course does.

This unique CCS course has an incredibly low subscription fee, making sure that every student who wants the extra coaching can afford it.

Although we have attempted to make this course as complete as possible, your motivation and the time spent in preparing for the exam play an equal role in determining your success on the real examination.

  • 41 classic STEP3 CCS cases
  • Continuous updates to the cases to comply with the exact Clinical Case Simulation (CCS) software keywords
  • Detailed Explanation for each case based on the most current evidence
  • Continuous addition of cases as needed based on the feed back
  • Key to Success and tips for the exam
